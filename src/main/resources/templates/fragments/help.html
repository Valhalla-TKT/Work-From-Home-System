Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for remote work?

To apply for remote work, click on the "Apply Form Now" button on the home. Fill in the required details and submit your application.

How do I navigate the home page?

The home page provides an overview of your activities and schedule. Use the calendar to keep track of important dates and deadlines.

Where can I find my form history?

You can access your previous form submissions by navigating to "Forms" > "Form History."

How do I view request form list?

Navigate to the "Forms" section and select "View request list" to see all the request forms that have been submitted to you.

How can I generate reports?

Generate and view reports of Work From Home System users by navigating to "Form" > "Ledger."

Where can I find the FAQs?

You can find answers to common questions in our FAQ section. Navigate to "Help" > "FAQs."

How can I contact support?

If you need further assistance, please contact our support team. Navigate to "Help" > "Contact Support."
